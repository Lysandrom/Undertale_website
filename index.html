General info about undertale

Undertale is a video game that was created and published by an indie developer called Toby Fox.
Toby Fox was responsible for scripting, designing and soundtrack and contains artworks from designer Temmie Chang and got 92/100 on Metacritics.
It was released on september 15th 2015. It was praised for its story, the characters, and the combat system by many game critics.
Undertale got selled over 1.7 million times and got nominated for awards such as game of the year.
The japanese studio 8-4 ported the game over to console: On the 15th of august 2017 Undertale got ported to the Playstation 4 and playstation vita
On the 18th of september 2018 it got ported to the Nintendo Switch and on march 16th it got ported to Xbox One and Xbox seriese x and series S
